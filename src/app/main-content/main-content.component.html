  • 2

    Add users

    Add users to the group

  • 3

    Add your payment details

    Add your payment

  • 4

    Start Group

    Click on start group. Every user should start the group to make it active.

  • 5

    Bid

    A user will be selected at random. You can choose to let the biddng window close to tranfer the money to the user or you can bid for an amount to forgo and get the money this month.

    Every user need to be a Superuser to bid and can bid for as many times in any group for 1 month. When the bidding window closes the user with the most money to forgo will win and will be declared winner for that month.

About ROSCA

The Rosca is a trusted and the first 'NO COMMISSION' social saving platform. You can create your group and invite other like-minded people especially friends and family from all over India and start saving or investing money. In times of need, you can also borrow money that is multiples of what you have invested by bidding. The Rosca gives you all the freedom to make your group according to your flexibility without any commission.
