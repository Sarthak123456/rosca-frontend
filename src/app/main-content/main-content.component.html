How it works?

 

  • 1
    Create your group

    You can create your group according to your flexibility by adding all the required information.

  • 2
    Add users

    After creating the group you can add users by their usernames or by sending them invite through WhatsApp.

  • 3
    Add your payment details

    Add your payment details to start the group. You can't go furthur without this.

  • 4
    Start Group

    Every user should start the group to make it active. After active you can either bid or transfer money to the randomly selected winner after the bidding window gets over.

About ROSCA

The Rosca is a trusted and the first 'NO COMMISSION' social saving platform. You can create your group and invite other like-minded people especially friends and family from all over India and start saving or investing money. In times of need, you can also borrow money that is multiples of what you have invested by bidding. The Rosca gives you all the freedom to make your group according to your flexibility without any commission.
